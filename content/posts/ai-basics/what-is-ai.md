# Artificial Intelligence (AI) Kya Hai?

Doston, aaj kal har jagah **AI** ki baat ho rahi hai. ChatGPT se lekar self-driving cars tak, AI hamari zindagi badal raha hai. Lekin sawal ye hai ki aakhir AI hai kya?

## AI ki Seedhi Definition
Simple shabdon mein kahein toh, **Artificial Intelligence** ka matlab hai machine ko "akalmand" banana. 

Jab hum kisi computer ya machine ko aise program karte hain ki wo insaano ki tarah:
1. **Seekh sake** (Learning)
2. **Decision le sake** (Decision Making)
3. **Galtiyon se sudhar sake** (Self-Correction)

...toh use hum AI kehte hain.

## Hum AI Kyun Use Karte Hain?
AI ka use isliye hota hai kyunki:
- Ye thakta nahi hai (24/7 kaam kar sakta hai).
- Ye bahut saara data ek saath process kar sakta hai.
- Ye boring aur repetitive kaamo ko fast kar deta hai.

## AI ke Kuch Examples
Aap rozana AI use kar rahe hain, shayad aapko pata na ho:
- **YouTube/Netflix Recommendations:** Jo aapko batate hain ki agla video kaunsa dekhna hai.
- **Google Maps:** Jo aapko sabse chota raasta batata hai.
- **Face ID:** Jo aapke chehre se phone unlock karta hai.
