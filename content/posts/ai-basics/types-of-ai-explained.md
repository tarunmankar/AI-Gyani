## Capabilities (Taakat) ke Aadhar Par Types of AI

Agar hum AI ko uski dimaagi taakat (capabilities) ke hisaab se baantein, toh ise hum teen main hisso mein divide kar sakte hain. 

Simple si baat hai, jaise ek chota baccha, ek bada insaan, aur ek super-genius scientist mein farq hota hai, waise hi in teen AI types mein farq hota hai:

1. **Artificial Narrow AI (ANI) - Weak AI**
2. **Artificial General Intelligence (AGI) - Strong AI**
3. **Artificial Super Intelligence (ASI)**

Ab in teeno ko ek-ek karke detail mein samajhte hain.

---

## 1. Artificial Narrow Intelligence (ANI) ya Weak AI

Iska naam 'Narrow' isliye hai kyunki iska focus sirf ek (narrow) kaam par hota hai. Ye usi kaam mein master hota hai, lekin uske baahar ise kuch nahi aata.

Maan lo aapne ek aisi AI banayi jo sirf ludo khelne mein champion hai. Wo AI duniya ke sabse best ludo player ko hara sakti hai. Lekin agar aap us AI se pucho ki "Bhai, 2+2 kitna hota hai?", toh wo blank ho jayegi. 

Aaj hum jis duniya mein jee rahe hain, wahan sirf aur sirf **Narrow AI** hi majood hai. Chahe wo kitna bhi advanced kyun na lage, wo hai Narrow AI hi.

### Narrow AI ke Real-Life Examples:
- **Siri ya Alexa:** Ye hamari baatein samajhte hain aur gaane play kar sakte hain, par khud se soch nahi sakte.
- **ChatGPT:** Ye text likhne aur sawalon ke jawab dene mein genius hai, lekin khud chal kar aapke liye paani nahi laa sakta.
- **Self-Driving Cars:** Tesla ki gaadiyan drive karne mein expert hain, lekin unhe chess khelna nahi aata.
- **Face Recognition:** Aapka phone jo aapki shakal dekh kar unlock hota hai.

**Dekh lo:** Narrow AI bilkul us student ki tarah hai jo sirf Maths mein 100/100 laata hai, par baaki sab subjects mein fail ho jata hai. 

---

## 2. Artificial General Intelligence (AGI) ya Strong AI

Ab aate hain level 2 par. Ise samajhne ke liye apne dimaag ko dekho. Insaan kitna smart hota hai! Hum ek hi din mein gaadi chala sakte hain, chess khel sakte hain, doston se mazak kar sakte hain, aur naya kaam seekh sakte hain.

**AGI ka matlab hai ek aisi machine jisme insaan ke barabar dimaag ho.** Ye ek ai system hoga jo kisi ek kaam mein nahi, balki har us kaam mein smart hoga jo insaan kar sakta hai.

Abhi tak duniya ke kisi bhi scientist ne AGI nahi banaya hai. Ye abhi sirf movies (jaise Iron Man ka JARVIS) ya research papers mein hi exist karta hai.

### AGI Kaise Kaam Karega?
- Ye aapse kisi bhi topic par bilkul insaano ki tarah baat karega aur emotions samajh payega.
- Agar ise koi naya kaam sikhana ho, toh iske liye alag se naya code nahi likhna padega. Ye khud seekh lega, jaise hum seekhte hain.
- Ye apne aas-paas ki duniya ko mehsoos kar payega aur uske hisaab se common sense use karega.

**Asli baat ye hai:** Duniya ki sabse badi AI companies, jaise OpenAI aur Google DeepMind, din-raat isiliye kaam kar rahi hain taaki wo pehla AGI bana sakein. Kuch logo ka manna hai ki hum aane wale 5 se 10 saalo mein AGI dekh lenge!

---

## 3. Artificial Super Intelligence (ASI)

Ye level thoda darawana bhi hai aur exciting bhi. **Super AI (ASI)** wo stage hai jahan machine insaan se bhi zyada smart ho jayegi. 

Socho ek aisi machine jiske paas Albert Einstein se zyada dimag ho, jise duniya ki har kitab muh-zabani yaad ho, aur jo nayi discoveries aise kare jaise hum baccho ke puzzles solve karte hain. 

Super AI hamari soch se itna aage hoga ki hum shayad uski baatein puri tarah samajh bhi na payein.

### Super AI Kya Kar Payega?
- Ye cancer aur dusri la-ilaaj bimariyo ka ilaaj kuch hi dino mein dhoond lega.
- Ye climate change jaisi badi problems ko solve karne ke naye tarike nikalega.
- Ye khud apne code ko behtar banayega aur khud se aage badhta jayega.

Lekin doston, Super AI ke saath risk bhi hai. Agar machine humse zyada smart ho gayi, toh kya wo hamari suneegi? Yahi wajah hai ki scientists abhi se AI Safety par kaam kar rahe hain. 

Ye abhi bahut door ki baat hai, shayad 30 se 50 saal baad ki!

## Functionality ke Aadhar Par Types of AI

Abhi tak humne dimaag ki taakat ke hisaab se baat ki. Lekin agar hum dekhein ki AI kaam *kaise* karta hai, toh iske 4 aur types hote hain:

1. **Reactive Machines:** Inke paas koi memory nahi hoti. Ye sirf current situation dekhte hain aur react karte hain. (Example: IBM ka Deep Blue jisne chess champion ko haraya tha).
2. **Limited Memory:** Ye apne purane experience (memory) ko thode time ke liye save karke naye faisle lete hain. (Example: Self-Driving cars jo aas-paas ki gaadiyo ko observe karti hain). Aaj ka zyadatar AI yahi hai.
3. **Theory of Mind:** Ye aisi machines hongi jo insaano ki feelings, beliefs aur emotions ko samajh kar interact karengi. Ye abhi development phase mein hai.
4. **Self-Aware AI:** Ye aisi machine hogi jise khud ki maujudgi ka ehsaas hoga (consciousness). Use pata hoga ki "Main kaun hoon". Ye ASI ke level ki baat hai aur abhi science fiction hi hai.

## Hum Insaan Abhi Kahan Khade Hain?

Agar hum in saare **Types of AI explained** ko summary mein dekhein, toh sachai ye hai ki hum abhi sirf **Narrow AI (Weak AI)** ki stage mein hain. 

Aapko lag sakta hai ki ChatGPT bahut general hai, lekin technical bhasha mein wo bhi Narrow AI hi hai kyunki use sirf "Text" samajhna aur likhna sikhaya gaya hai. Wo aapke liye chai nahi bana sakta.

Lekin ye safar bahut tezi se aage badh raha hai. Hum Narrow AI se General AI ki taraf bahut speed se badh rahe hain.

## FAQs (Aksar Puche Jane Wale Sawal)

### 1. Kya ChatGPT ek AGI (General AI) hai?
Nahi, ChatGPT ek bahut advanced Narrow AI hai. Ise sirf language process karna aata hai, ye insaano ki tarah har kaam nahi kar sakta ya real duniya ki common sense nahi rakhta.

### 2. Narrow AI aur General AI mein sabse bada farq kya hai?
Narrow AI sirf ek specific kaam mein expert hota hai (jaise gaadi chalana ya chess khelna). General AI kisi bhi kaam ko insaan ki tarah seekh kar kar sakta hai.

### 3. Kya Super AI insaano ko replace kar dega?
Super AI banne mein abhi bahut waqt hai. Jab wo banega, toh uska asar kaisa hoga ye kehna mushkil hai. Kuch log mante hain ye duniya ko bachayega, kuch kehte hain khatra hai.

### 4. Aaj hum kis type ka AI use karte hain?
Aaj hum jo kuch bhi use karte hain—Siri, Alexa, Google Maps, Netflix recommendations—wo sab Narrow AI (Weak AI) ka hissa hain.
