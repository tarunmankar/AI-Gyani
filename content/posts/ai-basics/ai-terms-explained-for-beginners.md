Aap internet par article padh rahe hote hain, ya kisi dost se baat kar rahe hote hain, aur achanak wahan koi kehta hai: *"Bhai, isme naya LLM model use hua hai jo NLP aur Neural Networks par kaam karta hai."*

Aur aapka reaction hota hai: *"Bhai, ye kis alien bhasha mein baat kar raha hai?"*

AI (Artificial Intelligence) ki sabse badi problem ye nahi hai ki wo mushkil hai, balki uske **Shabd (Vocabulary)** bahut bhaari hain. Scientists aur tech log aise words use karte hain jo aam insaan ke dimaag ke upar se nikal jate hain. 

Toh chaliye, is problem ka hamesha ke liye ilaaj karte hain! Aaj hum AI ke sabse common aur popular **"Jargon" (Bhaari shabdo)** ko apni natural Hinglish mein todenge. Ise apni 'AI Dictionary' samajhiye.

---

## 1. LLM (Large Language Model)
Ye word aapne ChatGPT aane ke baad bahut suna hoga. 
**Asaan Bhasha Mein:** LLM ek aisi computer program (model) hai jise internet par maujood laakhon kitabein aur websites padhai gayi hain (Large). Iska main kaam hai Insaano ki bhasha (Language) ko samajhna aur usi bhasha me jawaab dena. ChatGPT, Gemini, aur Claude ye sab LLMs hain.

## 2. Prompt Engineering
**Asaan Bhasha Mein:** AI se baat karne ki kala (Art). Aap AI ko jo "Command" ya sawal likh kar dete hain, use 'Prompt' kehte hain. Aur us Prompt ko itne acche tarike se likhna ki AI aapko best answer de, is skill ko 'Prompt Engineering' kehte hain.

## 3. Generative AI (Gen-AI)
**Asaan Bhasha Mein:** Wo AI jo kuch naya "Generate" (Paaida) kar sake. Normal AI Data ko analyze karta hai (jaise fraud pakadna), lekin Generative AI naya gaana bana sakta hai, nayi photo bana sakta hai (Midjourney), ya naya essay likh sakta hai (ChatGPT).

## 4. NLP (Natural Language Processing)
**Asaan Bhasha Mein:** Computer ko insaano ki bhasha (Hindi, English) samajhne aur bolne ke laayak banana. Jab aap Alexa ko bolte ho "Gaana bajao", toh Alexa NLP ka use karke hi samajhti hai ki aapke shabdo ka matlab kya hai.

## 5. Neural Network
**Asaan Bhasha Mein:** Insaani dimaag (Human Brain) ki nakal. Jaise hamare dimaag mein chote-chote neurons hote hain jo signal pass karte hain, wese hi computer ke andar bhi maths ke chhote-chhote formula ek doosre se jude hote hain data ko process karne ke liye. Ise Artificial Neural Network kehte hain.

## 6. Machine Learning (ML)
**Asaan Bhasha Mein:** Ek aisi technology jahan hum computer ko rules nahi sikhate, balki bahut saara data de dete hain, aur machine us data se apne aap seekh leti hai ki kya sahi hai aur kya galat. (Jise deep mein padhne ke liye aap hamari pichli post padh sakte hain!)

## 7. Deep Learning (DL)
**Asaan Bhasha Mein:** Ye Machine Learning ka bada bhai hai. Jab data bahut zyada complex ho (jaise kisi ki shakal pehchanna ya gaadi chalana), toh hum Deep Learning ka use karte hain. Ye hamesha 'Neural Networks' (dimaag wale networks) ka use karta hai.

## 8. Computer Vision (CV)
**Asaan Bhasha Mein:** Computer ko "Aankhein" dena. Wo technology jisse AI photos aur videos ko dekh kar samajh sakta hai ki usme kya hai. Aapke phone ka Face Unlock aur Tesla ki Self-driving car, Computer vision ka best example hain.

## 9. Hallucination (AI Hallucination)
**Asaan Bhasha Mein:** AI ka "Jhooth bolna" ya "Sapne dekhna". Kai baar AI ko answer nahi aata, lekin wo itne aatm-vishwas (confidence) ke saath galat jawab de deta hai ki lagta hai wahi sach hai. Ise AI Hallucination kehte hain.

## 10. Algorithm
**Asaan Bhasha Mein:** Khana banane ki Recipe! Computer ko diya gaya ek step-by-step mathematical formula jo use batata hai ki kisi problem ko solve kaise karna hai.

## 11. Turing Test
**Asaan Bhasha Mein:** Ye ek purana test hai jo check karne ke liye banaya gaya tha ki "Kya machine insaan jaisi smart ho gayi hai?". Agar koi machine aapse text par baat kare aur aapko pata na chale ki samne insaan hai ya computer, toh samjho usne Turing Test paas kar liya.

## 12. GPU (Graphics Processing Unit)
**Asaan Bhasha Mein:** Computer ka wo hissa jo AI ko chalane ke liye power deta hai. Pehle GPUs sirf Heavy Games khelne ke kaam aate the. Lekin AI ka data itna zyada hota hai ki normal CPU fail ho jata hai, isliye aajkal saari AI companies GPUs (jaise NVIDIA ke chips) kharidne me karodo kharch kar rahi hain.
