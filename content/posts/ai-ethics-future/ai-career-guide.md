"AI mein career banana chahta hoon — lekin kahan se shuru karoon?" 

Ye message mujhe roz milte hain. Aur honestly, samajh aata hai confusion — AI ka field itna vast hai ki pata nahi kaunsa rasta lein.

Aaj ki is guide mein main aapko clearly bataunga — **AI Career kaise start karein, kya seekhein, aur 2026 mein kaunse roles mein scope hai**.

---

## AI Field mein Kaunse Career Paths Hain?

AI ek single job nahi hai — ye ek poora ecosystem hai. Main roles:

### 1. Machine Learning Engineer
**Kya karta hai:** ML models design, train, aur deploy karta hai. Production-ready AI systems banata hai.

**Skills needed:** Python, Sklearn, TensorFlow/PyTorch, MLOps, Docker, Cloud

**Salary (India):** ₹8-30 LPA (experience ke hisaab se)

---

### 2. Data Scientist
**Kya karta hai:** Data se insights nikalta hai. Business problems ko data aur AI se solve karta hai.

**Skills needed:** Python, Pandas, SQL, Statistics, Visualization, ML basics

**Salary (India):** ₹6-25 LPA

---

### 3. Data Analyst
**Kya karta hai:** Data analyze karta hai, dashboards banata hai, reports deta hai.

**Skills needed:** SQL, Excel, Power BI/Tableau, Basic Python/R, Statistics

**Salary (India):** ₹4-15 LPA

*Best entry point for absolute beginners!*

---

### 4. NLP Engineer
**Kya karta hai:** Text aur language related AI systems banata hai — chatbots, sentiment analysis, translation.

**Skills needed:** Python, Hugging Face, Transformers, NLTK, SpaCy

**Salary (India):** ₹10-35 LPA

---

### 5. Computer Vision Engineer
**Kya karta hai:** Images aur videos process karne wale AI systems banata hai.

**Skills needed:** Python, OpenCV, CNNs, TensorFlow/PyTorch, Image processing

**Salary (India):** ₹10-35 LPA

---

### 6. AI Research Scientist
**Kya karta hai:** Novel AI algorithms aur models research karta hai. Papers publish karta hai.

**Skills needed:** Advanced Math, PhD preferred, Deep RL, Optimization theory

**Salary (India/Global):** ₹20-80 LPA (FAANG level aur upar)

---

### 7. Prompt Engineer
**Kya karta hai:** LLMs se best outputs nikalna, AI systems design karna.

**Skills needed:** Understanding of LLMs, communication skills, domain expertise

**Salary:** ₹8-25 LPA (new but growing role)

---

### 8. MLOps Engineer
**Kya karta hai:** ML models ki production lifecycle manage karta hai.

**Skills needed:** Docker, Kubernetes, CI/CD, Cloud (AWS/GCP/Azure), Python

**Salary (India):** ₹12-35 LPA

## Step-by-Step Beginner Roadmap

### Stage 1: Foundation (Month 1-2)
- ✅ Python basics seekho (variables, loops, functions, OOP)
- ✅ NumPy, Pandas, Matplotlib seekho
- ✅ Basic Statistics aur Probability

**Resources:** freeCodeCamp Python, AI-Gyani Python series

---

### Stage 2: Machine Learning Core (Month 3-4)
- ✅ ML algorithms (Regression, Classification, Clustering)
- ✅ Scikit-learn library
- ✅ Kaggle competitions shuru karo (Titanic from start)
- ✅ 1 complete project GitHub pe daalo

**Resources:** Kaggle Learn, Andrew Ng's ML Course (Coursera)

---

### Stage 3: Deep Learning aur Specialization (Month 5-6)
- ✅ Neural Networks, CNNs, RNNs basics
- ✅ TensorFlow ya PyTorch (ek choose karo)
- ✅ Apna domain choose karo (NLP/CV/etc.)
- ✅ 2-3 quality projects portfolio mein

**Resources:** Fast.ai, DeepLearning.AI Specialization

---

### Stage 4: Tools aur Production (Month 7-8)
- ✅ Git aur GitHub properly use karna
- ✅ Docker basics
- ✅ Cloud (AWS/GCP free tier try karo)
- ✅ Model deployment (Flask/Streamlit)
- ✅ MLOps basics

---

### Stage 5: Job Hunting (Month 9+)
- ✅ LinkedIn profile optimize karo
- ✅ GitHub portfolio strong karo
- ✅ Resume AI-tailored banao
- ✅ Apply karo (internships bhi theek hain!)
- ✅ Mock interviews practice karo

## FAQs

### 1. Kya engineering background zaroor hai AI career ke liye?
Ideal hai, lekin zaroor nahi. Career changers — doctors, lawyers, educators — bhi AI mein successfully aaye hain with right upskilling.

### 2. Kitna time lagta hai job-ready hone mein?
Seriously dedicated effort ke saath 8-12 months. Part-time pe 18-24 months.

### 3. Kya MBA wala AI mein aa sakta hai?
Haan! AI Product Manager, AI Strategy roles ke liye business background valuable hai.

### 4. First job ke liye minimum qualifications?
1-2 solid projects + Python + ML basics. Internship ya competition wins bonus hain.
