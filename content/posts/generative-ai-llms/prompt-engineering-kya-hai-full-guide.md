# Prompt Engineering: AI Se Sahi Kaam Kaise Karwayein?

Kya aap bhi ChatGPT se baar baar seedhe sawaal poochhte ho aur average sa jawab milta hai? Aur phir koi aapko batata hai ki unhe wahi AI se ek amazing article ya perfect code mil gaya?

Fark kya tha? **Prompt Engineering.**

Prompt Engineering wo kala hai jisme aap AI ko itni smartly instructions dete ho ki wo exactly wahi kare jo aap chahte ho. Aaj hum ye kala seekhenge!

![Prompt Engineering Guide](/images/prompt_engineering_guide.png)

## Prompt Engineering Kya Hai?

Simple definition: **AI ko diye jaane wale instruction (prompt) ko strategically aur systematically likhne ki kala.**

Ye aisa hi hai jaise ek junior employee ko kaam samjhana — agar aap vague bolo "kuch presentation bana do" toh result average hoga. Par agar aap clearly bolo "10 slides ki professional presentation banao, topic hai AI trends 2026, audience beginners hain, tone simple rakho" — toh result kaafi better hoga!

## Ek Achha Prompt Ke 4 Elements

### 1. Role (AI ko koi role do)
> "Tum ek experienced Python developer ho..."

AI ko ek identity dene se wo us context mein jawab deta hai.

### 2. Context (Background information do)
> "...main ek beginner hoon jo pehla script likhna chahta hoon..."

Jitna context, utna better answer.

### 3. Task (Exactly kya chahiye?)
> "...mujhe ek simple calculator program likhke do..."

Kaam clearly define karo.

### 4. Format (Output kaisa chahiye?)
> "...aur har line par comment lagao taaki mujhe samajh aaye."

Output ka format specify karo.

## Practical Prompt Examples

### Bura Prompt vs Achha Prompt

**❌ Bura:** "Blog post likho AI par"

**✅ Achha:**
> "Tum ek Hindi tech blogger ho. Mujhe ek 800 word ka blog post chahiye AI basics par. Audience: college students jo pehli baar AI padh rahe hain. Tone: simple, friendly, examples ke saath. Structure: intro, 3 main points, conclusion. Ek relatable analogy bhi use karo."

Dekha? Fark kitna bada hai!

## Top Prompt Engineering Techniques

### 1. Zero-Shot Prompting
Koi example diye bina seedha kaam bolna.
> "Is paragraph ka summary 3 bullets mein do."

### 2. Few-Shot Prompting
Kuch examples dekar pattern samjhana.
> "Ye examples dekho: [example1], [example2]. Ab usi pattern mein ye karo: [new task]"

### 3. Chain of Thought (CoT)
AI ko step-by-step sochne ko bolo.
> "Is problem ko step-by-step solve karo, har step clearly explain karo."

### 4. Role Play
AI ko ek specific role dena.
> "Tum ek strict grammar teacher ho. Meri writing ki mistakes dhundho aur explain karo kyun galat hain."

### 5. Iterative Prompting
Pehle draft lo, phir refine karo.
> "Pehle ek outline do. [AI deta hai] Ab isko poora article banao. [AI banata hai] Ab intro ko aur engaging banao."

## Common Prompt Engineering Mistakes

- **Too vague:** "Kuch achha likho" — AI ko pata nahi kya likhna hai
- **Too long context:** Ek prompt mein 10 alag kaam — AI confuse ho jaata hai
- **No format specification:** Format nahi bataya toh AI apni marzi se format choose karta hai
- **One shot expectation:** Pehle prompt se perfect result expect karna — iteration zaroori hai

### FAQs

**1. Prompt Engineering seekhne ke liye coding aani chahiye?**
Bilkul nahi! Ye ek language skill hai — clear thinking aur communication zyada important hai.

**2. Kya saare AI tools ke liye prompts same hote hain?**
Mostly similar principles kaam karte hain, lekin har model thoda alag respond karta hai. ChatGPT aur Claude ke best prompts slightly different ho sakte hain.

**3. Kya prompt engineering abhi bhi relevant hai?**
Haan, aur rahega. Chahe AI kitna bhi smart ho jaye, usse clearly communicate karna hamesha important rahega.

**4. Best prompt engineering resources kahan milenge?**
OpenAI ki official guide, PromptingGuide.ai, aur Anthropic ka prompt library — ye sab excellent sources hain.

**5. System prompt kya hota hai?**
Ye ek hidden instruction hoti hai jo AI ko conversation shuru hone se pehle di jaati hai — jaise "Tum hamesha formal language use karo." Ye ChatGPT Custom Instructions mein set hoti hai.
