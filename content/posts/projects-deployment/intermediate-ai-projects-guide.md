Beginner projects (jaise Titanic ya Iris) seekhne ke liye achhe hain, lekin wo aapko job dilane ke liye kafi nahi hain. Aaj ke time mein recruiters wo projects dekhte hain jisme **Deep Learning**, **Computer Vision**, ya **Large Language Models (LLMs)** ka use hua ho.

Is post mein hum 5 aise intermediate projects ke baare mein baat karenge jo aapki advanced skills ko prove karenge.

---

## 1. Face Mask Detector (Computer Vision)
CCTV footage ya webcam se real-time mein pehchanna ki kisne mask pehna hai aur kisne nahi.
- **Skills:** OpenCV, MobileNetV2, CNN.
- **Kyon banayein?** Isse aapki real-time image processing ki samajh dikhti hai.

## 2. Stock Market Price Predictor (LSTMs)
Pichle 10 saalo ka data dekh kar share ki price predict karna.
- **Skills:** Time Series Analysis, RNN, LSTM.
- **Challenge:** Stock market bahut volatile hota hai, isliye isme accuracy se zyada logic important hai.

## 3. Language Translator (Transformers)
Ek bhasha se dusri bhasha mein translate karne wala model (Jaise Google Translate ka chota version).
- **Skills:** Sequence-to-Sequence models, Attention mechanism.
- **Tools:** PyTorch ya TensorFlow.

## 4. Object Detection in Videos (YOLO)
Video mein objects (insaan, car, kutta, ped) ko detect karna aur unke charon taraf bounding box banana.
- **Skills:** YOLO (You Only Look Once) algorithm, Deep Learning.
- **Dataset:** COCO dataset.

## 5. Resume Parser (NLP)
Hazaron Resumes se automatically naam, phone number, skills aur education nikalna.
- **Skills:** Named Entity Recognition (NER), Spacy library.
- **Kyon banayein?** Ye HR departments ke liye ek real-world solution hai.

---

## In Projects ko "Job-Ready" kaise banayein?

Sirf code likhna kafi nahi hai. In baaton ka dhyan rakhein:
1. **Interactive UI:** Project ko Streamlit ya Flask ke sath connect karein.
2. **Cloud Deployment:** Ise Hugging Face ya AWS par deploy karein.
3. **Clean Code:** GitHub par achhi documentation aur comments likhein.
4. **Blog Post:** Apne project ke baare mein ek article likhein (Jaise aap abhi padh rahe hain!).

---

## Conclusion

Intermediate projects aapko ek "Data Scientist" banne ki raah par le jate hain. Inme se kisi ek par bhi agar aapne maharat (mastery) haasil kar li, toh aapka portfolio hazaron logo se alag dikhega.
