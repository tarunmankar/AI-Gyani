Aapne theory toh bahut padh li, lekin bina projects ke aap kabhi "Confident" feel nahi karenge. Ek beginner ke liye sabse badi chunauti hoti hai—**"Main kya banau?"**

Is post mein main aapko 10 aise project ideas de raha hoon jo na sirf asaan hain, balki inka data bhi internet par asani se mil jata hai.

---

## 1. Titanic Survival Prediction (Classification)
Ye Machine Learning ka "Hello World" hai. Aapko predict karna hai ki Titanic jahaz doobne par kaun bacha hoga aur kaun nahi.
- **Skills:** Data cleaning, Logistic Regression.
- **Dataset:** [Kaggle Titanic Dataset](https://www.kaggle.com/c/titanic)

## 2. Iris Flower Classification
Phoolon ki lambai aur chaudai (Petal/Sepal length) dekh kar batao ki wo kaunsi species ka phool hai.
- **Skills:** Basics of Scikit-learn.
- **Dataset:** Iris dataset (Sklearn library mein inbuilt hai).

## 3. House Price Prediction (Regression)
Ghar ka area, location, aur rooms dekh kar uski price predict karein.
- **Skills:** Linear Regression, Feature Engineering.
- **Dataset:** [Boston House Prices](https://www.kaggle.com/c/house-prices-advanced-regression-techniques)

## 4. Email Spam Classifier
Text analysis karke batao ki mail Spam hai ya Ham (Normal).
- **Skills:** NLP (Natural Language Processing), Naive Bayes.
- **Dataset:** [SMS Spam Collection](https://archive.ics.uci.edu/ml/datasets/sms+spam+collection)

## 5. Movie Recommendation System
Netflix jaisa system jo user ki pasand ke hisaab se movie suggest kare.
- **Skills:** Correlation, Content-based filtering.
- **Dataset:** [MovieLens Dataset](https://grouplens.org/datasets/movielens/)

## 6. MNIST Digit Recognition
Haath se likhe hue numbers (0-9) ko pehchan'ne wala model.
- **Skills:** Computer Vision, Neural Networks (CNN).
- **Dataset:** MNIST (Keras/PyTorch mein inbuilt hai).

## 7. Heart Disease Prediction
Patient ki health reports dekh kar predict karein ki use heart disease ka khatra hai ya nahi.
- **Skills:** Classification, Data Visualization.
- **Dataset:** [Heart Disease UCI](https://www.kaggle.com/ronitf/heart-disease-uci)

## 8. Customer Churn Prediction
Telecom ya Banking data dekh kar batao ki kaunsa customer company chhod kar ja sakta hai.
- **Skills:** Handling Imbalanced Data, Random Forest.
- **Dataset:** [Telco Customer Churn](https://www.kaggle.com/blastchar/telco-customer-churn)

## 9. Sentiment Analysis on Twitter
Log kisi topic ke baare mein kya soch rahe hain—Positive, Negative ya Neutral?
- **Skills:** TextBlob, NLTK, API integration.
- **Dataset:** Twitter API ya Kaggle sentiment data.

## 10. Simple Chatbot
Rules par chalta hua ek chatbot jo basic sawalo ke jawab de sake.
- **Skills:** Python If-Else ya Simple Pattern Matching.

---

## Conclusion

In 10 projects mein se kisi bhi 2-3 ko chunein aur unhe poora karein. Sirf code copy na karein, balki har step ko samjhein. Ek baar aap beginner projects kar lenge, toh hum **Intermediate Projects** ki taraf badhenge.
