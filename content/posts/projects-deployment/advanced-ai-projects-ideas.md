Agar aap 2026 mein AI ki duniya mein apni jagah banana chahte hain, toh aapko **Generative AI** aur **Large Language Models (LLMs)** ke sath kaam karna aana chahiye. Aaj ke time mein "Advanced" ka matlab hai—Aise systems banana jo sirf data analyze na karein, balki naya content generate bhi karein.

Is post mein hum 5 aise "Cutting Edge" projects ke baare mein baat karenge jo aaj ke tech giants (Google, Meta, OpenAI) bana rahe hain.

---

## 1. Custom PDF Chatbot (RAG - Retrieval Augmented Generation)
Aapne ek 500-page ki PDF upload ki, aur ab aap usse sawal puch rahe hain. AI us PDF ko padhkar jawab de raha hai.
- **Skills:** LangChain, Vector Databases (Pinecone/Chroma), OpenAI/Llama API.
- **Tech:** RAG architecture.

## 2. AI Image Generator App
User ek text likhega (Prompt), aur aapki app use ek beautiful image generate karke degi.
- **Skills:** Diffusion Models (Stable Diffusion), API integration.
- **Frontend:** Streamlit ya React.

## 3. Autonomous AI Agent for Web Research
Ek aisa bot jise aapne kaha "Best laptops under 50k search karke ek report banao". Wo khud Google karega, links padhega, aur report taiyaar karega.
- **Skills:** AutoGPT, BabyAGI, Agentic workflows.
- **Kyon banayein?** AI Agents future hain.

## 4. Real-time Sign Language Translator
Webcam se hand gestures dekh kar unhe text mein badalna (Behre aur goonge logo ki madad ke liye).
- **Skills:** Mediapipe, Deep Learning, Real-time processing.
- **Tech:** Computer Vision + NLP.

## 5. Personalized AI News Aggregator
Aapke interest ke hisaab se internet se news uthana, unhe summarize karna, aur aapko ek audio format mein sunana.
- **Skills:** Web Scraping, LLM Summarization, Text-to-Speech (TTS).

---

## Advanced Projects ki Chunautiyan (Challenges)

1. **Cost:** GPT-4 ya Gemini ki APIs use karne par paisa lagta hai. Iska solution hai **Open Source Models** (Llama 3, Mistral) ka use karna.
2. **Hardware:** In models ko run karne ke liye achha GPU chahiye.
3. **Accuracy:** AI hallucinations (galat baatein bolna) ko control karna ek bada kaam hai.
